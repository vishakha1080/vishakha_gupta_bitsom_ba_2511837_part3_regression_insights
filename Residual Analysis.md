\# Residual Analysis

\## Objective

Residual analysis was performed to evaluate the predictive performance of the final multiple regression model.

Residuals were calculated using:

Residual = Actual Monthly Sales − Predicted Monthly Sales

\---

\## Understanding Residuals

A positive residual indicates that actual sales were higher than predicted by the model.

A negative residual indicates that actual sales were lower than predicted by the model.

Large residual values suggest that additional factors not included in the model may be influencing store performance.

\---

\## Largest Positive Residuals

The records with the highest positive residuals significantly outperformed model expectations.

Possible explanations include:

\- Highly effective local promotions

\- Strong store management

\- Higher customer loyalty

\- Local market advantages

These observations suggest that some successful business practices are not fully captured by the variables included in the model.

\---

\## Largest Negative Residuals

The records with the highest negative residuals performed below model expectations.

Possible explanations include:

\- Operational inefficiencies

\- Product stockouts

\- Increased local competition

\- Lower customer demand

These observations indicate that additional operational or market-related variables may be influencing sales performance.

\---

\## Business Interpretation

The final regression model achieved an R² value of 0.8127, indicating that approximately 81.3% of the variation in monthly sales is explained by the selected predictors.

While the model performs well overall, residual analysis demonstrates that unexplained variation still exists.

The model may under-predict exceptionally high-performing stores and over-predict low-performing stores.

Future models may be improved by including variables such as:

\- Seasonal demand

\- Competitive intensity

\- Local economic conditions

\- Store management quality

\---

\## Conclusion

Residual analysis confirms that the final regression model provides strong predictive performance while highlighting opportunities for future model enhancement.
